About

DroneTracker is a drone-following script for NC State's AERPAW drones.

It controls AXIS cameras supporting VAPIX to track MavLink/ArduPilot drones and record video of the drones.

Its features include

  • Tailored for AERPAW's need

  • Can zoom to fit drone in view or with a certain amount of space around it

  • Can handle drone software crashes or disconnects and quickly recover

  • Integrates perfectly with Kafka for detailed telemetry and information.

  • Works with any AXIS PTZ camera supporting VAPIX!

This project is currently in active use and is supported
